Stop & Lock


Project information

  • Category: Prototyping
  • Client: Students
  • Role: PM and Designer
  • Youtube: Video
  • Website: stopandlock.co.uk

Design, manufacture and test a quickly deployable, secure and non-damaging lock for doors where fitting a lock is not allowed (rental accommodation bedroom).

This project under went 10s of prototyping iterations. Developed rapid prototyping, motor control and audino skills.
